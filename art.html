Logo Design

Over this past summer of 2020, I designed logo concepts for Delivery On Call (DOC), a small food delivery service for healthcare workers that has yet to be actualized. The first step was creating a set of different concepts to show my client Dom. After our meeting, I developed the final design on the bottom. I used a light shade of blue on the take-out bowl in honor of our healthcare workers on the front lines. This was done using Procreate.
